Illegal drugs seized in first-ever raid of Helldemption Island

By Perry Scope • Sep 07, 2025

Cocaine, Speed, and Pot. A devastating revelation at the center of Helldemption Island.

Production and storage are key elements that makes Helldemption Island thrive. However, a few police reports from local residents to 'The Obsidian Watch' led investigators to a visitation at the old potion house. The potion house normally produces the island's health potions and fire resistance for combat missions. But, something more sinister was brewing underneath. A massive bow fight conducted when investigators knocked on the door. After one officer was non-fatally shot, the alleged brewing master was shot down with 15 arrows.

In the basement of the potion house, a shulker box containing illegal drugs for mass distribution was discovered. Investigators reassure that there is no reason for local residesnts to be alarmed as they cordon the building to safely remove the drugs.

"That building is usually empty. What if kids went inside and found those drugs? Someone could have died."

— Virge, local resident

Investigators are agressively pursuing the organizers behind the distribution of these produced items. 'The Obsidian Watch' condemns the inattentive 'Diamond Division' for not following up on the first police report presented. Along the realm, there has been more advocacy for stricter drug control. This raid could be the leverage needed to make new policy changes.
